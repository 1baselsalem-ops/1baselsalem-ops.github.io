Technical overview

From responsibility
to working system.

Basel established Shifa's medical, safety, and design requirements. He defined the user-interface needs, medication-compartment specifications, average pill-size considerations, and the conditions required for controlled single-pill dispensing.

He led the physical prototype build by arranging and mounting internal components, assembling the dispensing mechanism, completing wiring and soldering, and safely integrating the motors, sensors, control boards, and power modules.

During integration, he tested each section independently, measured electrical outputs, inspected connections, diagnosed faults, and improved the reliability, insulation, routing, and protection of the internal wiring.
