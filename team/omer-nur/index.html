Technical overview

From responsibility
to working system.

Omer designed Shifa as a fully 3D-printed PETG enclosure with four independent hoppers. Each hopper uses a two-revolution screw cap to provide deliberate caregiver access while helping shield medication from environmental changes.

The dispensing mechanism uses a rotating disc with a precisely sized pocket and fixed stripper lid to isolate one pill per turn. A chute guides the pill past a break-beam sensor, while an offset shaft separates motor heat from the medication and a support disc carries the axial load.

He also designed serviceable fastener connections, passive stack-effect cooling, and the critical clearances through tolerance-stack analysis to create a quiet, robust, and maintainable bedside device.
