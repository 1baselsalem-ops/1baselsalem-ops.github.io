Technical overview

From responsibility
to working system.

Abdelaziz developed Shifa's web-based monitoring interface, turning dispensing data into an accessible view for caregivers and system administrators. The website provides a clear digital link between patient medication plans and the physical dispenser.

He organized patient and medication information so users can review schedules, follow dose activity, and inspect medication logs. By connecting the website to Firebase, changes and dispensing records can move between the monitoring interface and the wider Shifa system.

His work focused on making technical system data understandable and useful, giving caregivers a practical way to track patient adherence without being beside the device.
