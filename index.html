Shifa

Why it matters

A calmer routine for patients and caregivers.

Many medication errors happen because schedules are hard to remember, especially when several medicines are taken at different times. Shifa turns that routine into a guided workflow: load medicines into labeled compartments, program the schedule, dispense automatically, and keep a digital record.
