01 · The project

A simpler, safer medication routine.

Shifa was created to reduce the uncertainty caused by missed, delayed, or incorrect medication doses.

The problem

Medication schedules can be difficult to manage.

People who take several medicines, especially older adults and busy users, may forget a dose or take it at the wrong time. Caregivers can also have limited visibility of what happened.

Shifa combines a physical dispenser with connected software to make medication routines clearer and more reliable.

01

Timely

Dispenses the scheduled medicine at the correct time.

02

Simple

Uses an easy touchscreen and admin interface.

03

Connected

Stores schedules, logs, and alerts for remote monitoring.

The solution

One compact system.

Shifa has four medicine compartments, a touchscreen, automated dispensing, and a connected dashboard. Together, these parts help deliver the right scheduled dose and record the event.
